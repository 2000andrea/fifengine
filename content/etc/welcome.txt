Welcome to the F.I.F.E. Demo Release.
Toggle the Console with F10.

Move around the map with cursor keys.
r - Toggle roof visibility,
o - Toggle object visibility,
a - Toggle roof transparency,
c - Toggle coordinate visibility,
s - Increase roof alpha level,
x - Decrease roof alpha level,
+ - Increase global gamma level,
- - Decrease global gamma level,
e - Cycle through elevations.

--- Have fun :) ---

You can now actually use the lua console a bit.
Try mapview.setXPos(0) or console.print(...) for example
